Final Examination Schedule Released

Posted on: May 5, 2025
By: Examination Committee
Views: 238

The examination schedule for the second semester 2024/2025 academic session has been released. All students are advised to check their timetables and prepare accordingly.

Important Information

The examinations will commence on May 20, 2025 and end on June 5, 2025. All students are required to adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Students must arrive at the examination venue at least 30 minutes before the scheduled time.
  • Valid student ID cards must be displayed at all times during the examination.
  • Electronic devices, except approved calculators, are strictly prohibited in the examination hall.
  • Students with outstanding fees will not be allowed to sit for the examinations.
  • If you have any examination clash in your timetable, report to the department office immediately.

Examination Timetable

The detailed examination timetable is available in the attached PDF document. Please download and review it carefully.

Special Arrangements

Students requiring special arrangements due to medical conditions or disabilities should contact the Examination Office as soon as possible with supporting documentation.

Study Resources

The department library will remain open extended hours (8:00 AM - 10:00 PM) from May 13 to June 5, 2025, to facilitate student study sessions. Additionally, all course materials and past examination papers are available on the department e-learning portal.
